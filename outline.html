CSS Outline

An outline is a line drawn outside the element's border.

  1. outline-style
  2. outline-color
  3. outline-width

1.outline-style

Input

    <p style="outline: dotted;">Lorem ipsum dolor, sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Quaerat, deleniti!</p>
    <p style="outline: dashed;">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Totam, cupiditate.</p>
    <p style="outline: solid;">Lorem, ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Unde, sapiente?</p>
    <p style="outline: double;">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Reprehenderit, saepe.</p>
    <p style="outline: groove;">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Recusandae, corrupti!</p>
    <p style="outline: ridge;">Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Dolor, voluptate!</p>
    <p style="outline: inset;">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur, adipisicing elit. Vitae, delectus?</p>
    <p style="outline: outset;">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Non, nesciunt?</p>

Output

Lorem ipsum dolor, sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Quaerat, deleniti!

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Totam, cupiditate.

Lorem, ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Unde, sapiente?

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Reprehenderit, saepe.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Recusandae, corrupti!

Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Dolor, voluptate!

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur, adipisicing elit. Vitae, delectus?

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Non, nesciunt?
