    2.String Length:-

    To find the length of a string, use the built-in length property:-

    Input:-

            <p id="dome"></p>

        <script>
            let tex = "ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXY";
            document.getElementById("dome").innerHTML = tex.length;
